Gas paid = value routed to the L1

Burn counts only the deflationary slice (and, for ETH, only post-2021). Total gas paid (every unit of blockspace the app's activity demands) is the cleaner measure of the value an app routes to its chain, and it counts the whole life. Uniswap paid 108 ETH in gas in its first five months when its burn was still zero, and ~2.34M ETH over its life. Perpl has already routed 5.63M MON to Monad in ~5 months (5.44M of it burned; ~190K MON to validators as tips). Blockspace demand is the through-line: each of these apps is, first and always, a fee engine for its L1.

04

Cadence: the consensus that makes Monad the default trading chain

Cadence is Category Labs' next-gen consensus. Many proposers, no gatekeeper, blocks faster than the network delay. It's why the serious flow, and the collateral behind it, comes to Monad.

Many proposers, one block

Proposers build in parallel. A 2f+1 vote includes each. They merge into one block. No single party controls what's included, or in what order.

Censorship-resistant

One leader can drop your order. With many proposers, a correct one still includes it. The order lands. No leader-level front-running.

Sub-network-delay blocks · CEX-grade latency

Slots open every interval τ and overlap, so blocks never wait for each other and the interval drops below the network delay Δ. Speculative finality after one voting round. Quote, fill, and cancel at CEX-grade latency, fully onchain.

The collateral gravity well

Make Monad the best place to trade and it becomes the best place to park trading collateral. Every major bridges in, becomes Perpl margin, and burns MON on the way. Perpl is where that flow lands (§03, §05).

Cadence: a multi-concurrent-proposer BFT protocol (n = 3f+1) with block intervals below the network delay, from Category Labs (Babel, Elsheimy, Heimbach, et al.). Consensus-design claims cited to the Cadence protocol description; the collateral-migration thesis is forward-looking.

▸ Reference query: Perpl total gas & burn, all routes (Dune 8031622) 
-- Perpl: Total Gas & Burn (ALL routes)
-- A transaction counts as Perpl gas iff it interacted with the exchange:
--   UX/API = sent straight to the exchange (forwarder-relayed or direct)
--   SDK/SC = sent to a market-maker DelegatedAccount that emitted an exchange event
-- A `to = exchange`-only view (e.g. a gas-by-protocol query) sees just the UX/API third.
WITH tagged AS (
  SELECT
    CASE WHEN t."to" = 0x34B6552d57a35a1D042CcAe1951BD1C370112a6F
         THEN 'UX / API (forwarder + direct)'
         ELSE 'SDK / smart-contract (DelegatedAccount)' END AS path,
    CAST(t.gas_used AS double) AS gas_used,
    CAST(t.gas_used AS double) * CAST(t.gas_price AS double) AS fee_wei,
    t.success
  FROM monad.transactions t
  WHERE t.block_time >= TIMESTAMP '2026-01-01'
    AND ( t."to" = 0x34B6552d57a35a1D042CcAe1951BD1C370112a6F
       OR EXISTS ( SELECT 1 FROM monad.logs l
                   WHERE l.tx_hash = t.hash
                     AND l.contract_address = 0x34B6552d57a35a1D042CcAe1951BD1C370112a6F
                     AND l.block_time >= TIMESTAMP '2026-01-01' ) )
)
SELECT
  COALESCE(path, 'TOTAL: all Perpl gas') AS path,
  COUNT(*)                        AS n_tx,
  ROUND(SUM(gas_used) / 1e12, 2)  AS gas_trillions,
  ROUND(SUM(fee_wei) / 1e18, 0)   AS fee_mon,
  ROUND(SUM(gas_used) * 1e-7, 0)  AS burned_mon,
  COUNT_IF(NOT success)           AS n_revert
FROM tagged
GROUP BY ROLLUP (path)
ORDER BY gas_trillions DESC
